About Us

Our Mission

To carry on the business of designing, developing, and operating digital platforms, software systems, and web and mobile applications for solar, renewable, and other energy sources and allied technologies, including asset management solutions for renewable power plants.

Integration

To connect and integrate stakeholders of the renewable energy ecosystem, including installers, customers, nodal agencies, utilities, financial institutions, service providers, and government bodies, through secure digital platforms enabling coordination, communication, and data exchange.

Innovation

To develop and deploy software tools for real-time field data capture, remote monitoring, analytics, automated reporting, asset lifecycle management, predictive maintenance, and decision support for solar and other renewable energy projects.

Ecosystem

To establish and operate online digital ecosystems for the implementation, operation, maintenance, and cleaning of solar and renewable power plants, including associated products and services.

Sustainability

To develop digital solutions and applications for energy awareness, efficiency optimisation, renewable energy adoption, sustainability, customer engagement, and solar power plant cleaning management.

Expertise

To provide professional, scientific, technical, research, advisory, consulting, and support services relating to renewable energy, asset management, plant operations, solar cleaning solutions, digital transformation, data analytics, automation, artificial intelligence, machine learning, Internet of Things (IoT), and allied technologies.
